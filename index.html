The Tech


Just like a dessert's nutritional label, OnlyFlans will tell you exactly what's in your Flan. We won't be revealing exactly what's in each flan ID before minting (because the surprise is part of the fun in the dessert!) but we're big believers in transparency, so we've set up a way for you to cryptographically verify that the ingredients going into each flan don't change after you mint!


You can download this zip file, which contains the SHA256 sums for the metadata of every flan that can be minted, assigned by id. So once you mint your flans, you can compute the SHA256 sum of its metadata and ensure that it's the same as it was before you bought it.


Because the SHA256 hash algorithm has strong collision resistance, this cryptographically guarantees that nothing about your Flan could have been changed after minting.
